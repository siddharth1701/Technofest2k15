TechnoFest 2k15 computer science department

TECHNOFEST -2015 will be a sound platform for the students to exhibit their technical expertise, innovative imaginations coupled with executing capabilities. Contests will promote team work, co-ordination and zeal of R & D aptitude along with application skills which is inherent to the Engineering curricula. Around 2,000 students participate in this annual event

Guru Nanak Institutions is organizing a National Level Student Technical Challenge in Paper Presentation, Project Presentation & Poster Presentation (TECHNOFEST) from 2001. GNI is organising Technofest for the past 13 years.

Also several workshops, technical and hobby related events will be conducted along with this event. Students from engineering streams viz., ECE, EEE, CSE, IT, ME, CIVIL, AERO & PHARMACY branches are invited to submit entries to the respective Department Conveners in the prescribed registration format. The group has been spreading the light of education in the lives of thousands of students.
